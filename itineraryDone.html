Your itinerary:

Saturday starting at 3p.m.

Begin your Adventure at the Railroad Museum at 3:15p.m.

Start your day by visiting the Galveston Island Railroad Museum. This museum showcases the rich history of the railroad and its significance to the island. Explore the exhibits, historical artifacts, and learn about the island's role in the development of the railroad. It's a fascinating experience that provides insight into the island's history.

Leave for dinner reservations at Gaido's for 5p.m.

Head to Gaido's Seafood Restaurant for a delicious seafood lunch. Located right by the Gulf of Mexico, Gaido's offers fresh seafood with a fantastic view. Enjoy a seafood platter, Gulf shrimp, or one of their specialty dishes. It's a perfect spot to savor the island's culinary delights.

Begin Dinner at 5:30p.m.

Return back at the Meridian at 7:30p.m.
